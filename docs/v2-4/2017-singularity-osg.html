Singularity on the Open Science Grid

Jun 19, 2017 /

Can you imagine millions of containers, flying at hyperspeed across the continental grid for science? That’s not so far from the vision of the Open Science Grid. One of the members of the OSG, FabrIc for Frontier Experiments (FIFE), has written an article about how Singularity containers can be used on the OSG resources. Check it out here, and remember, the next time you see something flying in the sky, you might want to add “container” to your list with bird and plane.
